Yimin Zhao


I am a PhD student doing robotics research at National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Dr. Yeoh Ker-Wei, Justin. I am interested in applying machine learning methods in robotics, especially in autonomous vehicles.

Interests: Machine Learning in Robotics; Neural Network; Autonomous Vehicles;

Bio: I graduated as a BSc of Computer Science from University of Leeds (UoL). During that time, I focused on the researches of applying machine learning methods on EEG analysis to control robot and solve mental health issues. After that, I took a MSc of Robotics in NUS, and researched on autonomous driving supervised by Prof. Marcelo H. Ang Jr. in Advanced Robotics Centre.
